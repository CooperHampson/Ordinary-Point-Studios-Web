Ordinary Point Studios

Ordinary Point Studios is a hardware and software focused business. We currently pursue the field of game development, however we also run a hardware shop for all your computer or server hardware needs. OPS is also currently researching into the development and deployment of company specific software, and will soon be a service available for custom tailored software for companies both big and small.

Games

We focus on the development of micro games, often sold through steam. Small projects are the main focus point of the game development aspect of the business, although occasionally we will work on larger scaled projects. If you wish to see all the current games being developed and/or currently listed on steam, head on over to our Games tab on the sidebar. Each game we have developed is listed there along with all relevant information about the game such as what it is about, price and more.

Software (Coming Soon)

In the near future Ordinary Point Studios will be shifting its core focus from game development over to custom tailored software for companies. The software will be custom developed as per the customer's requests, and managed behind the scenes by Ordinary Point Studios. Usage of the software will be in the hands of the customer company, Ordinary Point Studios will only develop the software and update it as requested. The type of software made will be categorized into different tiers, each tier will contain a greater version of software and/or extra features. To find out more information and see what tiers will be available in future, look into the Software section.

Components Shop

Looking for a new component for your computer or server? Look no further, Ordinary Point Studios runs an Order-on-demand computer components shop. We sell a wide variety of different parts, and offer full computer build services as well for an additional fee. The shop is delivery only, however as soon as your order is palced we will strive towards getting it out to you in good condition as quick as possible. Wanting a bulk order? Ordinary Point Studios will take any bulk orders with a discounted price, however shipping fees and times will increase as the order size increases. For more information, visit our dedicated shop website at shop.ordinarypointstudios.com or by clicking the shop section on the sidebar.
